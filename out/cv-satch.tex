\section{\centerline{QUALIFICATION}}
\vspace{10pt}
\begin{multicols}{2}
A world-renowned guitarist, songwriter, and educator. Released over 15 studio albums and has won numerous awards, including a Grammy Award for Best Rock Instrumental Performance. Known for his technical skill and his ability to blend rock, blues, and jazz influences. He has influenced many other guitarists, including Kirk Hammett of Metallica and Steve Vai.        

Playing guitar at the age of 14. Studied with jazz guitarists Billy Bauer and Lennie Tristano. Taught guitar for 10 years, with students including Kirk Hammett and David Bryson of Counting Crows. In 1987, Released his debut album, "Not of This Earth." The album was a critical and commercial success, and it helped to establish a major force in the world of guitar.

Has continued to release successful albums throughout his career. Has also toured extensively, both as a solo artist and as a member of the G3 guitar tour, which also features Steve Vai and John Petrucci. A true guitar master, and his music continues to inspire and amaze audiences around the world.


\end{multicols}
